Our Journey

Harmony Sound was born from a simple yet profound belief: that exceptional audio has the power to transform everyday moments into extraordinary experiences. What began as a small passion project in a modest workshop has grown into a globally recognized name in premium audio.

Our founders, lifelong audiophiles and engineers, saw a gap in the market for audio equipment that didn't compromise -- devices that married pristine sound quality with timeless, elegant design. They set out to curate and create products that would satisfy even the most discerning ears.

Over the years, we've partnered with world-class manufacturers, invested in cutting-edge research, and built a community of music lovers who share our unwavering commitment to sonic perfection. Every product we offer has been rigorously tested and thoughtfully selected to meet our exacting standards.

A Legacy of Sound

Our History

2010 -- The Beginning

Harmony Sound was founded in a small workshop in Brooklyn, NY. Two engineers with a shared love for music began handcrafting custom headphones for local musicians and DJs.

2014 -- First Collection

We launched our first official product line, the "Flow" series, which was met with critical acclaim. Audio magazines praised the perfect balance of warmth and clarity in every pair.

2018 -- Going Global

Harmony Sound expanded internationally, opening showrooms in London, Tokyo, and Berlin. Our products were now available in over 40 countries, reaching audiophiles worldwide.

2021 -- Innovation Award

Our flagship "Flow Harmony" headphones won the coveted International Audio Innovation Award, recognized for their revolutionary adaptive noise cancellation technology.

2024 -- The Future

Today, we continue to push boundaries with our latest sustainable audio line and AI-powered sound personalization features, all while staying true to our founding principles.

Jonathan Hayes

Founder of Harmony Sound
